Rwanda eVisa FAQ

Who needs a Rwanda eVisa?

Most non-Rwandan nationals require an eVisa to enter Rwanda. Some nationalities can obtain visa on arrival. We will confirm your eligibility during assessment.

How long does Rwanda eVisa processing take?

Standard processing typically takes 5–7 business days. Our express service can reduce this to approximately 2–3 business days.

What documents are required?

Common documents include a valid passport, recent passport photo, travel itinerary, and proof of accommodation. Additional documents may be required depending on visa type.

Can you help with airport pickup and hotels?

Yes. We arrange reliable airport transfers and provide curated hotel options in Kigali and beyond.
