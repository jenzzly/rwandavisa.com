bestTimeTitle: Safari Options & Best Time to Visit
bestTimeText: Akagera offers game drives (morning, afternoon, and night), boat safaris on Lake Ihema, and guided walking safaris. The park has luxury lodges, tented camps, and budget-friendly accommodation near the southern entrance. The dry season (June-September and December-February) offers the best game viewing as animals gather near water sources.
